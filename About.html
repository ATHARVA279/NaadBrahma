About Us

Welcome to Naad Brahma: The Home of Rhythm and Melody



Established amidst the musical renaissance of the 1970s, Naad Brahma stands as a timeless testament to our unwavering passion for the tabla, sitar, and harmonium. For decades, our journey has been an ode to the enchanting rhythms and melodious tunes that resonate through the corridors of Indian music.

ABOUT OUR TEACHERS:


USTAD MANU ABRAHAM

Pandit Manu Abraham discovered his tabla talent at age three, and he's since become a renowned tabla player who blends classical Indian rhythms with global influences. His innovative teaching methods set him apart in the music world.


PANDITA SWATI MOHANTY

With her enchanting melodies and deep-rooted passion for harmonium, Pandita Swati Mohanty is a celebrated virtuoso. Swati's teaching style is both unique and effective, offering aspiring harmonium players a serene escape into the world of music.


PANDIT PARTH ZILE

Pandit Parth Zile is a sitar virtuoso known for his captivating performances. With a deep passion for the sitar, he has dedicated his life to mastering this classical Indian instrument. Parth's teaching style is both inspiring and effective, making the sitar accessible to aspiring musicians.
